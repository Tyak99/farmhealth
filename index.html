The Big Story

Our Mission

FarmHealth is a social enterprise birthed with the mission to improve the health of under-served population of farmers in Nigeria. We aim at providing and delivering lifesaving health information to farmers and their families via mobile phones and other easy to reach platforms

For under-served and deprived rural Farmers, FarmHealth would use the power of mobile technology to improve health care for Farmers in Nigeria by ensuring proper attention is given to them; through access to affordable and quality health services.

We will also aim at providing health care card for registered farmers with our partner’s hospitals and health care centers within the community.
