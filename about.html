About Us

Given that over 65% of Nigerians live in rural areas, it is easy to understand why most Nigerians do not have up to date information and financial access to basic healthcare services especially farmers with National Health Insurance Scheme covering below 5% in Nigeria.

FarmHealth is a social enterprise birthed with the mission to improve the health of under-served population of farmers in Nigeria. We aim at providing and delivering lifesaving health information to farmers and their families via mobile phones. We will also aim at providing health care card for registered farmers with our partner’s hospitals and health care centers within the community.

Health Information

We support farmers in both rural and urban areas with health information, reminders and medical advice in order to improve their health and that of their farm produce. We believe majority of farmers lack health access to health care service and information, FarmHealth will leverage on their access to mobile phone for reaching out to farmers with this basic needs

Why We are Different

Our platform is easy to access and adoptable mobile-based health information for farmers in both rural and urban areas. The option of indigenous language system for those who lack formal education especially for the rural farmers also makes us unique.
Apart from marketing our services majorly to the under-served and deprived markets/farmers, we are positioning ourselves to be forerunners in use of podcast, indigenous language options, call for health complain service, USSD and funky info-graphics to deliver our services to our two categories of users. Our services are distinct from our competitors.
